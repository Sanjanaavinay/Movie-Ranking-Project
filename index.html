My Top 3 Recent Favourite Movies(Malayalam)

Aavesham

Aavesham movie is one of my top tier recent favourites.As a malayali I'm not exaggarating but fafa's acting was excelllent.The movie is funfilled action which you can enjoy with ur family and friends. Ranga character played by fafa was cherry on top for this movie and also songs were full of vibe.Its Theatrical hit and I would rate this movie 9.5/10.

Manjummel boys

Manjummel boys left a footprint in malayalam cinema. Manjummel boys had a collection crossing 100Crores worldwide.It is a survival thriller which was based on real story ,the cinematography along with masterpiece song (kanmani anbodu kadhalan)just rewired that love song to friendship anthem everywhere. I would rate this movie 9.8/10.

Premalu

Premalu was a romcom filled with rollercoaster of emotions with a good balance of music,laughter,friendship,love all in one package took a place in people's heart. Naslen as sachin and meenu as mamitha is going viral in social media.The general audience had a treat of the bestromcom n feel goodmovie which will be cherished forever.I would rate this movie 9/10.
